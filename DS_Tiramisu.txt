Tiramisu - Joke

Menu Class: Dessert

Descript
Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert. Ladyfinger cookies dipped in coffee, then layered with mascarponed and dusted with cocoa powder.


1. In a medium bown beat together the egg yolks and 1/3 cup of sugar.
2mooth and set aside.
4. Dissolve remaining 2/3 cup sugar in coffee.
5. Quickly, to avoid complete saturation, dip ends of ladyfingers in coffee mixture.
6. Place ladyfingers in a single layer in a 9 x 13 inch glass baking dish.
7. Spread a layer of cheese mixture over the ladyfingers.
8. Repeat layers, ending with cheese mixture.
9. Cover and refrigerate for 3 hours.
10. Sprinkle with cocoa just before serving.
